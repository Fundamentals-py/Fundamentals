Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and courses

How long does a typical website project take?
The timeline varies based on complexity, but most standard websites take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to launch. More complex projects with custom functionality may take 6-8 weeks.
Do I need any prior experience for your courses?
Our Python course is designed for absolute beginners, while our Full-Stack Development course assumes basic familiarity with HTML and CSS. We offer preparatory materials for students who need them.
What ongoing support do you provide after project completion?
All our projects come with at least 1 month of free support. We offer various maintenance packages for ongoing needs, including updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization.
How do you ensure the quality of your training programs?
Our curriculum is developed by industry professionals and updated quarterly. We maintain small class sizes, provide hands-on projects, and offer 1:1 mentorship sessions to ensure each student's success.
What payment options do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and PayPal. For courses, we offer installment plans to make payments more manageable.
